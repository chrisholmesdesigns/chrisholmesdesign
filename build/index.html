ABOUT

It just so happens that I fell in love with design at the young age of 2. You should have seen the wall art I produced with finger paint… my mom was thrilled. Well, as I’ve gotten older, I’ve switched from wall art to web art and it’s lead me to UI/UX design. I place focus on influential UI that evokes emotion from the user. Human-centered design is my passion and I strive to make a seamless experience overall. I'm currently pursuing a Bachelors degree in Graphic Design at San Diego State University with a focus on designing for the internet and typography. Expected graduation is Spring 2019 and am looking for internships.

Outside of design, you can find me playing an intense game of ping pong or out on the bay kayaking. On weekends, you can often find me taking a ride on a motorcycle and trying to find the best sandwiches that America's finest city has to offer.
