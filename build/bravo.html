What Is Bravo News?

This mobile application allows users to see snippets of news article about Bravo reality shows. The app starts with a guided tour of how to set up daily reminders to check the news and moves on to showcase the articles in a clean and concise manner.

Target Audience

  • Female
  • Ages 24-38
  • Invested In Current Events

Influential Idea

Stay update and receive reminders for the latest celebrity news.

Role

UX Research & Design Execution

Category

Mobile UI Design

Research

User Personas

Challenge

Design an app that engages readers in an efficient way. The app should be clean and not be overly distracting while still maintaining brand identity. The information that readers seek out should be easily digestible and the ability to set reminders should be intuitive. The app should deliver what it promises without being overly complicated.

Solution

Streamlined the design to include branded icons and a tutorial that allows readers to get to their favorite gossip in a timely manner. The news articles are laid out in such a manner that the newest source is at the top which makes it easy for readers to feel like they are up-to-date and in-the-know.
